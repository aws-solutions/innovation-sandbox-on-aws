---
title: Assign Lease
---

Create leases directly on behalf of other users without requiring approval. Useful for workshops, training sessions, and controlled distribution scenarios.

---

## Wizard Steps

**Step 1: Select Lease Template**
Choose any available lease template (public or private).

**Step 2: Select User**
Enter the user's email address (must exist within AWS IAM Identity Center).

**Step 3: Terms of Service**
Accept terms on behalf of the assigned user.

**Step 4: Review & Submit**
Review details and optionally add comments for audit purposes.

---

For more information, refer to the [Assigning leases to users](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/solutions/latest/innovation-sandbox-on-aws/manager-guide.html#assigning-leases) section.
